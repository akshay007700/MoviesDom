Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about MoviesDom

How often is movie data updated?

Our movie data is updated in real-time through our integration with The Movie Database (TMDB) API. New releases and updates are typically available within hours of official announcements.

Is MoviesDom free to use?

Yes! MoviesDom is completely free to use. We believe movie information should be accessible to everyone. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

How can I contribute to MoviesDom?

You can contribute by writing reviews, rating movies, participating in discussions, and reporting inaccurate information. We also welcome feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes! MoviesDom is available as a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

How accurate is the movie information?

We strive for 100% accuracy by using official sources and community verification. Our data comes from TMDB, which is maintained by a global community of contributors and industry professionals.

Can I request features or report bugs?

Absolutely! We welcome feature requests and bug reports. Please use our contact form or visit our GitHub repository to submit your feedback.
